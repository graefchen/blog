Introduction to this blog

11. February 2024

So... Writing an Introduction to a Blog is way harder than I expected it to be... But here it comes:

This Blog is mainly aimed for me to better my understanding and knowledge of certain topics. Those can either be about video games, programming, computer science, linguistics, archaeology and any other interesting tidbits that I find. And I hope that you as a reader of this blog will enjoy the articles that I will publish here.

As someone might be interested in how this website might have been made, I am writing some parts of the process and thoughts down below:

Design: I aimed to make a blog more akin to the idea of the small web by Ben Hoyt. Furthermore I added dark and light modes, mainly because I personally prefer when a website changes its colour according to the visitors preferences.

Articles: I have many different interests as mentioned above. So I try to vary my selection of articles. I will not guarantee a fair selection, as this blog is mainly aimed at myself and improving my skills in writing and understanding the subject I am writing about, may it be about some (boring) things about archaeology, some cool technical topics or simply a rant.

Technology: I am using for this blog just plain old HTML and CSS and (currently) no JavaScript. This is very simple and works for me and the current scope this blog is in. Furthermore I do not use any cookies. For the use-case of this blog I do not see a very good reason why I should use blogs. There is no interactivity on this blog (at least for now).

I hope you enjoyed my first blog post. I hope to see you around.
